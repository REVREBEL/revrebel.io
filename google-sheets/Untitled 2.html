    **Navigate to the shared Canva link**
    In order to comment, Canva may require you to signup and login to your account but doing so is free and you have no obligations once your account is created. Tip: Login with a social account like Google to eliminate any additional steps like filling out your name & email.
    **Navigation within Canva**\ The bottom slide bar provides easy navigation to review to jump from slide to slide. You can start at Slide #1 and transition to the next slide by pressing the arrow key.
    Zoom Saved
    **Commenting or Sharing Feedback**\ Located in the top right corner, click on the message icon to open up the commenting window for the slide.
    Zoom Saved
    Add your comment for any changes that you'd like to see made. Bonus: If you like or hate the slide with any emotion in between, adding a carefully selected emoji says it all.
    **Submit the Comment**\ Once you've added your comments, make sure you submit feedback by clicking on the purple comment button at the bottom of the box.
